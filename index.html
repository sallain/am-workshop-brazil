About Us

Sara Allain, Systems Archivist

  • Joined Artefactual in January 2016 to do community support, requirements analysis, user documentation, and QA testing for Archivematica and AtoM.
  • Previously worked as an archivist and librarian in special collections, digital scholarship, and communications at the University of Toronto and Ryerson University.
  • Also involved in: Islandora project, Archives Association of Ontario, Code4Lib BC.

About Us

Artefactual Systems

Founded in 2001 by Peter Van Garderen.

Small software development company (< 20 people) based near Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada.


Artefactual Systems

We also have remote workers in Victoria (Canada), Buffalo (USA), Valencia (Spain), and Santiago de Compostela (Spain).

We are archivists, developers, systems administrators, and DevOps professionals.

FOSS

We make free, open source software for heritage sector institutions.

Our clients are universities, public libraries, cities, financial institutions, charities, private organizations, and more.
